About Benjamin

Hi My name is Benjamin Hargrave and I'm a junior programmer, aspiring to be working in the games industry. I finished my study at Academy of Interactive Entertainment, Sydney where I studied game development specializing in programming. Afterwards then went overseas for a break and some ideas.

Why Games?

Games have always been a big part of my life, when I was younger I was more into sport, however as I grew up I became to love subjects like mathematics, over time I grew to love the adventure and possibilities that games could bring and online games was like a big social network where you could work together with your friends. In my last few years of school I started modding in Warcraft 3, by the time I finished School I had a good idea of what I wanted to do.

Experience

Languages proficient in: C, C++, C#, GLSL, HLSL.

Languages with experience: Java, Javascript, Lua, Visual Basic, HTML, CSS.

Libraries/APIs used: DirectX, OpenGL, OpenCL, PhysX, Bullet, FMOD.

Engines/Frameworks: Gamebryo, Unity, SDL Framework, XNA, Unreal.

Tools: Visual Studio, Maya, Perforce, Git, Photoshop.
